Introduction

This document outlines the requirements for a new, robust compliance automation platform specifically designed for African businesses. The current market is dominated by global products with prohibitive pricing and generic, often manual, processes that do not cater to the unique regulatory and business landscape of Africa.

This platform will provide an affordable, intuitive, and context-aware solution, starting with the Nigerian market and expanding across the continent. Non-Functional Requirements & Scope

Key Requirements

  • Security: All sensitive data (API tokens, passwords) must be encrypted at rest and in transit.
  • Scalability: Architecture must support thousands of companies without performance degradation.
  • Availability: The platform must have an uptime of at least 99.9%.
  • Localization: Must support multiple currencies (NGN, KES, ZAR, USD) and be architected for future language support.

Out of Scope for MVP

  • Full GRC features beyond information security (e.g., financial compliance, ESG).
  • A public marketplace for auditors.
  • A dedicated mobile application.
  • On-premise deployment options.
